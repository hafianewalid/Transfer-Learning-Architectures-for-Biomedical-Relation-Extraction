Further, effects of a stronger beneficial association between n-3 PUFA in RBCs and plasma lipid parameters- including lower totalcholesterol (TC), lower low-density lipoprotein cholesterol (LDL-C) or higher high-density lipoprotein cholesterol (HDL-C) concentrations- were associated with AGT M235T (rs699) TT genotype, CETP G-971A (rs4783961) AG genotype, T allele carriers of CETP C-4502T (rs183130), and T allele carriers ofCETP Ile405Val (rs5882).
