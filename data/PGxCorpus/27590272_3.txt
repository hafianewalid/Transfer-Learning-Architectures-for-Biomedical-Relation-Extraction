Four important transporter genes are expressed in the kidney , including organic cation transporter 2 ( OCT2 ) , multidrug and toxin extrusion 1 ( MATE1 ) , ATP-binding cassette subfamily B member 1 ( ABCB1 ) , and ATP-binding cassette subfamily C member 2 ( ABCC2 ) , and genetic polymorphisms in these genes may alter the efficacy and adverse effects of platinum drugs .
