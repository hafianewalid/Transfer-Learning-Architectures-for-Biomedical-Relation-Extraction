Successful results that have been achieved within the field of pharmacogenomics so far are , to name a few , HLA-B * 5701 screening to avoid hypersensitivity to the antiretroviral abacavir , thiopurine S-methyltransferase ( TPMT ) genotyping to avoid thiopurine toxicity , and CYP2C9 and VKORC1 genotyping for better dosing of the anticoagulant warfarin .
