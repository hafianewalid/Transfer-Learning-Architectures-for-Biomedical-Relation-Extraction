We genotyped 23 single nucleotide polymorphisms ( SNPs ) in the PDYN and OPRK1 genes in 816 alcohol -dependent subjects and investigated their association with : ( 1 ) negative craving measured by a subscale of the Inventory of Drug Taking Situations ; ( 2 ) a self-reported history of depression ; ( 3 ) the intensity of depressive symptoms measured by the Beck Depression Inventory-II .
