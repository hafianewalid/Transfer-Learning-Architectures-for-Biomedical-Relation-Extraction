Adjusted odds ratios ( OR ) for skin lesions were estimated in relation to the polymorphisms in the glutathione S-transferase omega1 and methylenetetrahydrofolate reductase genes , the percentage of monomethylarsonous acid ( % MMA ) and dimethylarsinic acid ( % DMA ) in urine , and the ratios of MMA to inorganic arsenic and DMA to MMA .
