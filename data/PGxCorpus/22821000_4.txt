We examined the role of Ranbp2 haploinsufficiency on cellular and metabolic manifestations linked to tyrosine -hydroxylase ( TH ( + ) ) dopaminergic neurons and glial cells of the brain and retina upon acute challenge to 1-methyl-4-phenyl-1,2,3,6-tetrahydropyridine ( MPTP ) , a parkinsonian neurotoxin , which models facets of Parkinson disease .
